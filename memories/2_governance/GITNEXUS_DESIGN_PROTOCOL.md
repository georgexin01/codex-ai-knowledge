# 🛡️ GITNEXUS DESIGN PROTOCOL (V1.0)

## 🎯 The Purpose
Gitnexus ensures that our high-density design iterations are safely managed. It prevents "Design Drift" and ensures that the **Master Blueprint** remains the source of truth.

## ⚖️ Governance Rules

### 1. The Experimental Branching (Design Sandboxing)
- **Rule**: Every major redesign (e.g., "Step 01-13 Mission") MUST be treated as a logical branch.
- **Action**: Use Gitnexus to track changes to `custom.css` and `template/*.php` separately from the "Stable" core.
- **Safety**: If a "Randomized" design fails to "Wow" the user, Gitnexus allows for an immediate **Volcanic Purge** (reverting to the last Crystal state).

### 2. The Handshake Commit
- **Rule**: No design mission is complete until the `walkthrough.md` is generated and "Sovereign-Approved" by the User.
- **Action**: Every `walkthrough.md` serves as the "Changelog" for the Gitnexus design pulse.

### 3. Blueprint Synchronization
- **Rule**: Gitnexus monitors the `BLUEPRINT.md`. If the Blueprint's "Primary Objective" changes, Gitnexus triggers a **DNA Re-alignment** in all active design missions.
